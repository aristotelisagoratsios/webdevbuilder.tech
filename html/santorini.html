A few words about Santorini

Santorini is essentially what remains after an enormous volcanic eruption that destroyed the earliest settlements on a formerly single island, and created the current geological caldera. A giant central, rectangular lagoon with steep cliffs on three sides. The main island slopes downward to the Aegean Sea.
